Our Vision

Our philosophy is that a good student will go on to become a great person. We therefore aim to provide a communication service that will help all elementary schools shape more good students so that the world can have more great people.

Our Mission

We strive to create the best communication system for primary schools, by ensuring every student's guardian can access notifications on any mobile device.

We strive to build a system that serves in the teacher's best interest; always giving teachers control over work entering into their personal lives.
